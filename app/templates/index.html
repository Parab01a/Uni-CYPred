-----  Uni-CYPred  -----

Cytochrome P450 (CYP) is a widely distributed enzyme family in living organisms, that is involved in many important biological transformation reactions, including drug metabolism, xenobiotic detoxification, hormone synthesis. Inhibition of CYP activity can affect drug metabolism in the body, leading to serious drug-drug interactions. Therefore, it is of great guiding significance to predict potential CYP inhibitors in the drug development quickly and accurately. Uni-Pred is currently focused on CYP2B6 and 2C8, which are responsible for approximately 7.2% and 4.7% of clinical drug metabolism, respectively. Uni-Pred is dedicated to help researchers to conduct reasonable and reliable compound pre-screening in the early stages of drug development.
